Role Summary:
A Full Stack Developer is responsible for building and enhancing web applications and experiences for customers.
Key Responsibilities:
- Design and develop responsive and interactive web pages using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.
- Implement dynamic functionality using Node.js and Express.js.
- Maintain, troubleshoot, and optimize databases and SQL queries.
- Collaborate with front-end and back-end teams to ensure application integration and performance.
Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
- Demonstrable experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and SQL.
- Strong understanding of RESTful APIs and data structures.
- Experience with Git and Agile methodologies.
